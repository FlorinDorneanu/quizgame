Game Rules

  • The game has a set of 10 consecutive general knowledge questions!
  • The answer time for each question is 10 seconds.
  • If you do not answer the question within 10 seconds, you will be moved on to the next question.
  • After choosing an answer, the green color appears when the answer to the question is correct and the red color when the answer is incorrect.
  • After you send a correct or incorrect answer, you will be directed to the next question automatically.
  • The one who answers correctly in the shortest time to as many questions as possible wins!
